Welcome To Ropes 2017

Welcome to the Muskoka Woods 2017 ropes team!

It's great to have you guys on board and it's set to be an exciting Summer.I can't wait to meet you all in person and work with you throughout the season! Muskoka Woods is an incredible place to spend your Summer. If you've worked here before, welcome home. If this is your first time working here you're about to discover something really special. I hope this season that we create an awesome community of instructors and a tight team.

About Me

Hi, I'm Tim Davidson, your Area Head for this season! I was born and raised in Northern Ireland, but also lived in England for eight years and Canada for one. I currently study Interaction Design at Belfast School of Art and Design. Basically it's graphic and web design (in case you hadn't already guessed) with a little bit of magic. When I'm not studying I work in NI's only 5 Star hotel, it's pretty sweet, I also do some freelance graphic design. Outside of work I really enjoy listening to music, watching TV shows and movies, hanging out with people, and water sports like sailing and kayaking.

This is my third Summer at Muskoka Woods but my fifth season. Last year I had the opportunity to spend my gap year working on site as an instructor. During that time I got my Ropes Level 1 and Level 2 and instructed before joining the Summer high ropes staff, so I got to play about on the course a lot. It's a cool job and you can have some wild fun!

The Team

Everyone has been chosen carefully because of their abilities and their personalities. If you're feeling nervous before coming up to camp, don't be, you'll fit right in. Just remember, Muskoka Woods chose you and you're here for a reason! As well as staff week, you guys will be going through ropes training to gain your Level 1. This is a great time to become a team and get to know each other over late nights reading green binders and tying endless amounts of knots in rope that's almost too short to tie knots in! What's awesome is that you guys get a qualification that can be used in a job when returning home after camp, your Level 1 qualifies you for a year. So make the most of it, quit school and go work at Treetop Trekking for a year!

I can't wait to see everyone bond together as a team at the ropes shed. Bring something unique to you that will add to the positive vibe of the team and the Muskoka Woods community! You'll be surprsised at the people you meet, the friends you leave with and the things you learn whether that be a new climbing technique or which drink combinations are the best in the dining halls (if you mix them all, it's something quite special)!
